INTRODUCTION                                    *bpm*

Mappings for auto-scrolling.

                                                *<C-UP>
<C-UP> Vertically scroll upwards by one line

                                                *<C-DOWN>
<C-DOWN> Vertically scroll downwards by one line

                                                *<C-RIGHT>
<C-RIGHT> Horizontally scroll right by one line

                                                *<C-LEFT>
<C-LEFT> Horizontally scroll left by one line

                                                *<leader>-<C-UP>
<leader><C-UP> Continuously vertically scroll upwards

                                                *<leader>-<C-DOWN>
<leader><C-DOWN> Continuously vertically scroll downwards

                                                *<leader>-<C-RIGHT>
<leader><C-RIGHT> Continuously horizontally scroll rightwards

                                                *<leader>-<C-LEFT>
<leader><C-LEFT> Continuously horizontally scroll leftwards
